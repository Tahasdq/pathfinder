# 🧭 Pathfinder AI: The Career Intelligence Engine

**Pathfinder AI** is a professional-grade career transformation platform designed for the modern engineer. It bridges the gap between traditional software development and the frontier of AI Engineering by providing personalized, data-driven evolution strategies and immersive simulation environments.

---

## 🚀 The Core Vision
In an era of rapid technological shift, engineers often find themselves "trapped" in traditional stacks while the industry pivots toward Generative AI. Pathfinder AI acts as an **autonomous career architect**, analyzing your current profile and synthesizing a precise, multi-month strategy to reach senior AI Engineer/Architect roles.

---

## 🛠️ Key Features

### 1. **AI Strategy Synthesis**
Using **Gemini 1.5 Pro**, Pathfinder analyzes your target role and generates:
*   **Skill Gap Radar**: A high-fidelity visualization of exactly where your current proficiency falls short of market demands.
*   **Technical Architecture**: Dynamic, auto-generated **Mermaid.js** diagrams showing the cloud infrastructure (GCP/Vertex AI) you need to master.
*   **Strategic Roadmap**: A month-by-month evolution plan with actionable milestones.

### 2. **Immersive Interview Simulator**
A voice-enabled, low-latency interview environment where you practice with a "War Room" aesthetic:
*   **Persona-Driven**: Questions range from high-level architecture to deep-dive coding logic.
*   **Real-time Critique**: Receive instant feedback on your answers, including a numeric "Readiness Score" and qualitative improvement tips.
*   **Voice/Text Hybrid**: Integrated Web Speech API for a seamless "hands-free" simulation experience.

### 3. **The Command Center (Analytics)**
A persistence-driven dashboard that tracks your growth over time:
*   **Trajectory Charting**: Interactive **Recharts** Area Charts showing your Readiness Score history.
*   **Session Intelligence**: Complete logs of every past simulation, including AI critiques and question history, securely stored in MongoDB.

### 4. **Adaptive Cloud Stack**
Deep integration with **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)** documentation, providing specific service recommendations (Vertex AI, BigQuery ML, AlloyDB) tailored to your specific technical path.

---

## 💻 Technical Architecture

*   **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
*   **AI Engine**: Google Gemini API (Pro & Flash)
*   **State Management**: Zustand (Client-side)
*   **Persistence**: MongoDB & Mongoose
*   **Authentication**: NextAuth.js (Google OAuth 2.0)
*   **UI/UX**: Tailwind CSS 4.0, Framer Motion (Animations), Lucide Icons
*   **Visualizations**: Recharts (Analytics) & Mermaid.js (Architecture)
